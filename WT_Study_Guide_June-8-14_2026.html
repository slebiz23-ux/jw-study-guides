🔍 How Would You Answer?

Why can we trust in “the God of truth”?

We can trust in Jehovah for three interconnected reasons. First, as the Creator, he is the Source and Sustainer of all life—in complete control of the universe from the beginning and always will be. Second, because of his love—he created us to share life, gave us free will, prepared a beautiful home, and provided his Son as a ransom when things went wrong. Third, because of his track record—he is “the Fulfiller of all that he promises.” From the Promised Land to the Messiah to the worldwide preaching work, Jehovah’s promises always come true. His very personality is inseparable from truth—making him the most reliable Person in the universe.

Jehovah began bringing an end to Satan’s world in 1914, when Jesus cast Satan and his demons to the earth. World conditions have worsened since then—exactly as Bible prophecy foretold. Meanwhile, right now, Jehovah is accomplishing his purpose through the greatest preaching work in history—gathering honesthearted people from every nation, with angels directing the effort, so that all lovers of truth can survive into the new world.
